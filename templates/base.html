About This Project

This tool was originally created for my mom, to help her catalog her mother's artwork in a way that could be easily shared with others.

Believing that artists and collectors everywhere could benefit from a simple, organized system, I decided to make this project public. Hopefully, it can help others preserve and share an artistic legacy.
